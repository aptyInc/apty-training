Statue of Unity

Statue of Unity is dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel-India's first Deputy Prime Minister and a key leader in India's independence movement.He is known as the "Iron Man of India" for uniting over 560 princely states into a single nation.

The Location of Statue of Unity on Google Maps

The Statue of Unity is located in Kevadia, Narmada district, in the state of Gujarat, India. It stands near the Sardar Sarovar Dam on the Narmada River, approximately 200 kilometers southeast of Vadodara.
