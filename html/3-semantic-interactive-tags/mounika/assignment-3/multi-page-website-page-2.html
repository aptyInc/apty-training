About Our Forest

Our lush forest embodies multiple layers—from the canopy to the soil—creating a living web of support.

Forests are powerful climate allies:

  • They store huge amounts of carbon—especially in soils and roots—helping slow climate change.
  • Only about 20% of Earth's forests remain intact; preserving old-growth forests—less than 7% of US forests—is vital.
  • Eco-bonuses: Trees cool cities via shade and transpiration.
