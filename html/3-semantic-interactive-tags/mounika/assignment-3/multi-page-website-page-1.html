Welcome to the Forest!

Explore the beauty, learn about its secrets, and get inspired to protect our green allies.

  • Did you know forests cover 31% of the Earth's land area? That's over 4 billion hectares.
  • They host more than 80% of land-based biodiversity—animals, plants, and insects make their home here.
  • More than 55% of drinking water in the US comes from forests, cleaning and regulating water systems.
