Action

The action genre is a popular genre in film, literature, and video games that features fast-paced, intense, and thrilling sequences of physical feats, combat, and excitement

Thriller

Thriller is a genre of fiction that creates a sense of suspense, excitement, and anticipation in the audience. Thrillers are often plot-driven and action-packed, and they can include elements of crime, horror, and detective fiction.

Science Fiction

Science fiction is a genre of speculative fiction that explores the impact of science and technology on society and humanity.

Horror

The horror genre is a type of literature, film, and television that aims to scare, shock, or repulse audiences. Horror stories often explore the unknown and tap into our most primal fears, such as: vulnerability, nightmares, alienation, fear of death, and fear of sexuality.
