Description:

The music video for J. Cole's "Middle Child" is a visually striking representation of his position in the rap game. The video opens with J. Cole navigating a dark, empty hallway, symbolizing the solitude and tension of being "in the middle" — neither the new guard nor the established legend. He stands in the intersection of two worlds, all while reflecting on his journey and the shifting dynamics of the rap industry. As the video unfolds, we see a blend of surreal, artistic scenes, like Cole rapping on top of a mountain and through a barren desert, capturing the theme of balancing between being grounded and aiming for greater heights. Throughout, Cole’s introspective lyrics emphasize his role as a bridge between generations of hip-hop artists, and the video adds an extra layer of visual storytelling to his message of self-awareness, ambition, and mastery.
