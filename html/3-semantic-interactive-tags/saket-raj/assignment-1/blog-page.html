This blog explores how artificial intelligence is shaping the world — from academic publishing to business and decision-making.

Author

The academic world has long been adapting to new technologies, often with a mix of fascination and skepticism... Generative AI, like so many tools before it, promised to ease the heavy burden of intellectual work — and now we’re seeing its impact in one of the most traditional and ritualized corners of the scientific ecosystem: academic journals and their peer-review processes.

Despite its widespread lack of familiarity, AI is a technology that is transforming every walk of life. It is a wide-ranging tool that enables people to rethink how we integrate information, analyze data, and use the resulting insights to improve decisionmaking...
