A Short story

Once upon a time, in a cozy little forest, lived a clever rabbit named Rosie. She loved to hop and play, but she was also known for her quick wit and problem-solving skills. One sunny morning, while exploring near the river, Rosie noticed a group of squirrels struggling to reach a pile of nuts on a high branch. The nuts were too far up, and the squirrels couldn't jump high enough to get them. Rosie, seeing their dilemma, had an idea. She quickly gathered some long, sturdy vines and tied them together, creating a rope. Then, she carefully climbed a nearby tree and tossed the rope down to the squirrels. With Rosie's help, the squirrels were able to pull themselves up to the branch and enjoy the delicious nuts. The squirrels were overjoyed and thanked Rosie for her cleverness and kindness. From that day on, Rosie became known as the "Helper Rabbit" of the forest, always ready to lend a paw or a vine to those in need.
