The Lost Puppy

One sunny morning, Lily was walking through the forest when she heard a soft whimper. Curious, she followed the sound and found a tiny puppy, shivering under a bush. The puppy had no collar and looked hungry and scared. Lily gently picked him up and whispered, "Don’t worry, I’ll take care of you." She took him home, gave him warm milk and a soft blanket, and named him "Buddy""Buddy". From that day on, Lily and Buddy became the best of friends.
