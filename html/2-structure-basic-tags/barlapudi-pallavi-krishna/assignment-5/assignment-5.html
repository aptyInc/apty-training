Apty

Apty is a Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) that helps businesses guide users through enterprise software and optimize business processes. It's a software company founded in 2018 by Krishna Dunthoori, based in Austin, Texas. Apty's platform uses AI to provide in-app guidance and automation, aiming to improve user experience and software adoption.The platform uses artificial intelligence to offer in-app guidance, helping users understand and complete tasks within software applications.
