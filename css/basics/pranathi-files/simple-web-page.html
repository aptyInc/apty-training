A beach is a landform along the shoreline of an ocean, sea, lake, or river, typically consisting of sand, pebbles, or rocks. Beaches are dynamic environments, constantly shaped by natural forces like tides, waves, and weather.

Sandy Beaches

  • Sandy Beaches Made of fine sand (quartz, shells, coral).
  • Soft and smooth, popular for swimming and sunbathing.
  • Example: Miami Beach(USA).
