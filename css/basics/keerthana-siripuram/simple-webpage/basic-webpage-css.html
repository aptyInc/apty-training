Too Good to Be True by Carola Lovering is a gripping psychological thriller filled with twists and suspense. The story revolves around Skye Starling, who believes she has found her perfect love in Burke Michaels. However, as their whirlwind romance intensifies, dark secrets begin to surface. Told from multiple perspectives and timelines, the novel explores themes of obsession, betrayal, and the lengths people go to for love and revenge. With unexpected revelations at every turn, Too Good to Be True keeps readers guessing until the final page.
