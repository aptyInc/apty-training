What is Customer Handling?

Customer handling refers to the strategies and techniques used to manage interactions with customers effectively. It involves understanding customer needs, providing solutions, and ensuring satisfaction.

Key Strategies

    • Active Listening
      • Empathy and Patience
        • Clear Communication
          • Quick Issue Resolution

Benefits of Effective Customer Handling

Proper customer handling leads to improved customer satisfaction, loyalty, and retention. It also helps businesses build a positive reputation and long-lasting relationships.
